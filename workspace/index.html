The Cleanup

Clippers, shears, and razors are the basic tools that are used to perform a haircut. These are the tools that you should have in your kit as a new barber. You can purchase these tools from your local beauty supply store. Be sure to care for your tools so that they will last throughout your barbering career.

The cleanup aspect of barbering is very important. Make sure you have a sheen spray to finish off your client's cut, some aftershave lotion for post shaving, and, most importantly, alcohol for infection control. If you have these three items, then you're good to go as a beginner.
